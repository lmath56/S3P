‘I remember the needle hurt, everything went black, and the spinning woman cackled. Where is she?’

‘The disguised witch is gone. I modified her spell. In her version that needle prick would’ve killed you. Your parents forgot to invite her to your christening.’

‘Anyone can make a mistake. The witch didn’t believe in forgiveness then.’

‘Indeed. She won’t be experiencing it now either. The moment the Prince here kissed you so you woke, the witch was killed. Only way to ensure she couldn’t return.’

The Prince and Sleeping Beauty exchanged glances.

‘And people think fairies are nice…’ the Prince said.
